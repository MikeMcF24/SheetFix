Step by step
01

Describe the situation

Tell SheetFix what your spreadsheet does, what's going wrong, and what you need it to do. The form takes about 5 minutes.

An AI analyses your brief instantly and gives you a plain-language breakdown of what needs fixing and why — before you spend a cent.

Your description is processed via an AI tool. If your spreadsheet contains sensitive data, describe the issue in general terms or use dummy examples — do not paste confidential information into the form.

Free · No account needed · Takes ~5 minutes
02

Review and confirm

You get a plain-language AI analysis within seconds — what's wrong, what would fix it, and which tier fits. If anything's off, correct it and get an updated version. No commitment until you're happy.

If you're fixing an existing sheet, you'll send the file by email once you've confirmed the brief. SheetFix will review the scope and confirm the next step before work begins.

03

SheetFix builds and delivers

Once you confirm, SheetFix sends an invoice for the agreed tier. Work starts when payment clears — your finished spreadsheet is delivered by email within 2–3 business days.

Every file includes a Start Here tab, colour-coded inputs, and clean logic. Reasonable clarification and minor tidy-up after delivery are included — if something needs tweaking, it gets sorted.

Three flat-price tiers — $149, $299, or $499 incl GST. Not sure which fits? The free analysis tells you. See full pricing →

Common questions
What software do you work with?
Excel and Google Sheets. Both covered. Mention which one you use in the brief.
Do I need to share my actual data?
Not always. For fix jobs, SheetFix needs the file — but you can replace sensitive data with dummy values before sending. For builds from scratch, SheetFix works from your brief and uses sample data to demonstrate the logic.
How long does it take?
The free analysis is instant — you get it within seconds of submitting. Turnaround on the finished spreadsheet is 2–3 business days from when payment is received.
What if the delivered file isn't quite right?
Reasonable clarification and minor tidy-up are included after delivery — if something needs tweaking, it gets sorted.
Do you do ongoing work?
Not yet — but a maintenance subscription is coming. If you've got an ongoing need, get in touch and SheetFix can talk through what makes sense.
