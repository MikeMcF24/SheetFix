The process

Simple from your end.
Thorough from ours.

You fill in a brief, get a free instant analysis, and we take it from there. No back-and-forth calls, no jargon, no mystery.

Step by step
01

Fill in the brief

Tell us what your spreadsheet does, what's going wrong, and what you need it to do. The form takes about 5 minutes.

Our AI analyses your brief instantly and gives you a plain-language breakdown of what we'd fix and why — before you spend a cent.

Free · No account needed · Takes ~5 minutes
02

Confirm your analysis

Review the AI analysis. If something's off, you can correct it and get an updated version. Once you're happy, confirm — that locks in your brief and sends it through to us.

If you're fixing an existing sheet, you'll send us the file by email at this point.

03

Receive your instant analysis

Within seconds of submitting, you get a plain-language AI analysis of your spreadsheet situation — what's wrong, what would fix it, and which tier your job sits in.

Review it, confirm it's right, and if anything's off you can correct it and get an updated version. No commitment until you're happy.

04

Confirm and we get to work

Once you confirm the analysis, an invoice is sent for the agreed tier. Payment triggers the build — your finished spreadsheet is delivered by email within 2–3 business days.

Every file includes a Start Here tab, colour-coded inputs, and clean logic. One or two rounds of feedback are included — that's normal and expected.

Common questions
What software do you work with?
Excel and Google Sheets. Both covered. Tell us which one you use in the brief.
Do I need to share my actual data?
Not always. For fix jobs, we'll need the file — but you can replace sensitive data with dummy values before sending. For builds from scratch, we work from your brief and use sample data to demonstrate the logic.
How long does it take?
The free analysis is instant — you get it within seconds of submitting. Turnaround on the finished spreadsheet is 2–3 business days from when payment is received.
What if the V1 isn't quite right?
One or two rounds of feedback are included as standard — that's a normal part of the job, not a problem. If something's missed, it gets fixed.
Do you do ongoing work?
Not yet — but a maintenance subscription is coming. If you've got an ongoing need, get in touch and we'll talk through what makes sense.
