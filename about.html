About SheetFix

One person.
Your spreadsheet, sorted.

SheetFix is a small Wellington-based service run by one person, for small businesses that rely on spreadsheets but don't have the time, patience, or in-house skills to untangle them.

Where this came from

I build and fix spreadsheets for small businesses, sole traders, and anyone who's inherited a sheet they don't fully understand — or needs one made properly from a brief.

I've worked alongside businesses and trade organisations long enough to see the same pattern repeat: a spreadsheet that started simple, got added to by three different people, and is now something nobody fully understands — but everyone depends on.

The problem isn't the spreadsheet. It's that fixing it properly takes knowledge most people don't have time to develop. That's what SheetFix is for.

You describe the situation, get a free AI-powered analysis of what's wrong, and I build you something clean — with instructions, logic that makes sense, and a layout that doesn't require a manual to interpret.

Who this isn't for

I'll help anyone who comes across SheetFix — but it may not be the right fit for everyone, and it's worth being upfront about that.

  • Enterprise teams with IT departments and dedicated data analysts
  • Businesses that need database software, not spreadsheets
  • Complex integrations across multiple platforms and APIs
  • Anyone looking for ongoing weekly managed services (subscription coming later)
